ESACCI_GHG_L2_SCIAMACHY

Variables

The table below lists the variables that are present in the HARP product that results from an ingestion of ESACCI_GHG_L2_SCIAMACHY data.

field name type dimensions unit description
datetime double {time} [seconds since 2000-01-01] time of the measurement
surface_altitude double {time} [m] average surface altitude w.r.t. geoid
latitude double {time} [degree_north] latitude of the ground pixel center
longitude double {time} [degree_east] longitude of the ground pixel center
latitude_bounds double {time, 4} [degree_north] corner latitudes for the ground pixel of the measurement
longitude_bounds double {time, 4} [degree_east] corner longitudes for the ground pixel of the measurement
sensor_zenith_angle double {time} [degree] sensor zenith angle
solar_zenith_angle double {time} [degree] solar zenith angle
CH4_column_volume_mixing_ratio double {time} [ppmv] CH4 column volume mixing ratio
CH4_column_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time} [ppmv] CH4 column volume mixing ratio uncertainty
CO2_column_volume_mixing_ratio double {time} [ppmv] CO2 column volume mixing ratio
CO2_column_volume_mixing_ratio_uncertainty double {time} [ppmv] CO2 column volume mixing ratio uncertainty
index int32 {time}   zero-based index of the sample within the source product
